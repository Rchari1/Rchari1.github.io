Teaching Experience

As an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, I have contributed to various courses, enriching students' understanding in astrophysics and physics.

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy focuses on creating an inclusive and engaging learning environment, encouraging curiosity and exploration in the fields of physics and astrophysics. Students learn best when they are motivated by the subject, and I contribute best to this by offering supplemental lectures and harping on the importance of physics.
